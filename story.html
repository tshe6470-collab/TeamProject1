Our Story

At KHAANA, we believe that food is more than fuel. It’s a way to connect communities, share culture, and create memorable experiences.

Our History

Founded in 2015 as a family-run kitchen, KHAANA started serving small wellness events. With rising demand, we expanded to weddings, corporate gatherings, and large community festivals.
