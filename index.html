Our Story

At Khaana, we believe that food is more than fuel — it’s a way to bring people together, nourish communities, and create memorable experiences. Founded with a passion for healthy living and sustainable foodservice, Khaana has grown into a trusted catering company serving wholesome meals with a touch of creativity.

Our History

Khaana began in 2015 as a small family-run business offering fresh, home-style meals to local wellness events. With a growing demand for nutritious catering, we expanded our offerings to corporate gatherings, weddings, and community celebrations. Today, Khaana blends modern catering with timeless values: health, balance, and care.

Our Mission

We’re committed to making healthy food accessible and enjoyable for everyone. From farm-fresh ingredients to eco-friendly packaging, everything we do is designed with your well-being — and the planet’s — in mind.
